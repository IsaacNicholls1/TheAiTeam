About The Arcade

The Arcade is a collection of classic arcade games that you can play for free. We have games like Snake, Space Invaders

Our games are designed to be simple and fun to play. We hope you enjoy playing them as much as we did making them.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the joy of classic arcade games to a new generation of players. We believe that everyone should have access to fun and engaging games, regardless of their age or experience level.

That's why we've created The Arcade - a place where you can play all your favorite games for free, without any ads or in-app purchases. We hope you enjoy playing our games as much as we enjoyed making them!

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate developers who love classic arcade games. We believe that these games are timeless and deserve to be preserved for future generations to enjoy.
